About FishMart

Your trusted fish supplier

FishMart was established in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide fresh, high-quality fish to consumers while supporting local fishermen. We work directly with fishing communities around Lake Victoria and the Kenyan coast to bring you the best selection of fish and seafood.

Our modern facilities and cold chain logistics ensure that your fish remains fresh from the water to your doorstep. We're committed to sustainable fishing practices and supporting the local economy.
